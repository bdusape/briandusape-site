01. About Me

Hello! I'm Brian, a product manager with a passion for solving real-world problems through technology. My journey into product began with a fascination for how great user experiences can transform complex systems into something intuitive and delightful.

With a Master's in Information Systems from Drexel University, I've had the privilege of working with great teams at Valley Bank and AmeriSave Mortgage, where I've led the development of successful financial products and streamlined processes to enhance customer engagement.

My time in fintech revealed a critical insight: the most powerful leverage comes from automating inefficient workflows. This led me to found Luntra Labs, an early-stage venture focused on building AI-powered tools for overlooked operators. It's the next step in my journey to solve meaningful problems with technology.
